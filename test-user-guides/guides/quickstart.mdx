# Quickstart Guide

Get up and running in minutes with this step-by-step guide.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit the signup page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Verify your email address

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

Fill out these essential details:
- **Name**: Your display name
- **Company**: Your organization (optional)
- **Role**: Your job title or role

## Step 3: Explore the Dashboard

The dashboard includes:
- **Overview**: Key metrics and insights
- **Settings**: Customize your experience
- **Help**: Access to support resources
